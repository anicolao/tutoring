# Project Vision: AI Tutoring Companion

**Our Vision**  
To create a deeply engaging and wildly efficient learning experience by bridging the gap between live tutoring and independent study through intelligent AI companionship.

## The Core Concept
The traditional tutoring model relies heavily on the tutor manually identifying gaps, assigning homework, and reviewing it. Our reimagined approach introduces an AI that listens in on live tutoring sessions. By analyzing the organic conversation, the AI precisely maps the student's knowledge gaps. 

After the session, the AI collaboratively works with the tutor and student to assign targeted work and confirm that those specific gaps are successfully closed.

## Why This Matters

### For the Student
- **Engaging & Fast-Progressing:** Students experience a highly customized learning path without wasting time on concepts they already understand.
- **Continuous Support:** Learning doesn't stop when the session ends. The AI acts as an always-available guide for out-of-session work.

### For the Tutor
- **Maximum Value & Efficiency:** The AI handles the repetitive tasks of diagnosing gaps and generating homework. The tutor arrives at the next session knowing exactly where to focus their valuable face-to-face time.
- **Insightful Analytics:** Tutors gain a detailed breakdown of the student's independent progress.

## The Workflow
1. **Listen:** The AI securely processes the audio of the live session.
2. **Diagnose:** It identifies the student's conceptual gaps and specific struggles.
3. **Assign:** The AI proposes independent work/exercises for the student (which the tutor can review and approve).
4. **Evaluate:** As the student completes the work, the AI evaluates performance and determines if the gap is truly closed.
5. **Report:** The AI briefs the tutor on the student's progress ahead of the next live session.
