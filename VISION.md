# Project Vision: AI Tutoring Companion

**Our Vision**  
To create a deeply engaging and wildly efficient learning experience by bridging the gap between live tutoring and independent study through intelligent AI companionship.

## The Core Concept

The traditional tutoring model relies heavily on the tutor manually identifying gaps, assigning homework, and reviewing it. Our reimagined approach introduces an AI that processes both the audio and video stream of live tutoring sessions. By analyzing the organic conversation alongside the shared visual workspace (like a digital whiteboard for math problems), the AI precisely maps the student's knowledge gaps based on their spoken questions and their on-screen work.

After the session, the AI collaboratively works with the tutor and student to assign targeted work. Rather than static worksheets, problem sets are completed through an interactive session directly with the AI, which helps the student solve the problems step-by-step and confirms that those specific gaps are successfully closed.

## Why This Matters

### For the Student

- **Engaging & Fast-Progressing:** Students experience a highly customized learning path without wasting time on concepts they already understand.
- **Continuous Support:** Learning doesn't stop when the session ends. The AI acts as an always-available guide for out-of-session work.

### For the Tutor

- **Maximum Value & Efficiency:** The AI handles the repetitive tasks of diagnosing gaps and generating homework. The tutor arrives at the next session knowing exactly where to focus their valuable face-to-face time.
- **Insightful Analytics:** Tutors gain a detailed breakdown of the student's independent progress.

## The Workflow

1. **Observe & Listen:** The AI securely processes both the audio and the visual workspace (e.g., a shared whiteboard) of the live session.
2. **Diagnose:** It identifies the student's conceptual gaps by correlating their verbal explanations with their written work.
3. **Assign & Interact:** The AI proposes problem sets for the student (which the tutor can review). Instead of doing these autonomously, the student carries out an interactive session directly with the AI to solve the problems with its help.
4. **Evaluate:** As the interactive session concludes, the AI evaluates performance and determines if the core conceptual gaps have truly closed.
5. **Report:** The AI briefs the tutor on the student's progress ahead of the next live session.
